live for real

Saturday, 6 August 2022

Do you have any dreams? Goals? Targets?

Are you working hard enough to achieve them?

While the productivity folks might tell you to optimize each second of your life, you are missing away most of it.

Let me tell you something.

The life which you are living today was once your dream. Do you see it? If not complete, I am sure at least half of it.

Why do you not savour this? Why is there no gratitude but only the fire to get ahead in this race?

Take a break. From your goals. And your smartphone. Spend some time reflecting on this beautiful life of yours. Enjoy a good meal with your family. Take a walk in park. See the sunshine. And live for real!
